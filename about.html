How It All Started

Bake + Brew began in 2019 with a simple mission: to serve the Wembley community with fresh, quality food and exceptional coffee. Located just outside Wembley Stadium on Market Square, we've become a beloved spot for locals and visitors alike.

We wanted to create a welcoming space where you could grab a quick bite before a match, meet friends for coffee, or simply enjoy fresh HALAL food made with care. Whether you're heading to the stadium or the OVO Arena, we're here to fuel your day.

Today, Bake + Brew is proudly rated #1 in Wembley on TripAdvisor. We serve everything from fresh HALAL pasties and jacket potatoes to sandwiches, paninis, sausage rolls, and premium coffee drinks. Open on all event days — we're your go-to spot in Wembley.

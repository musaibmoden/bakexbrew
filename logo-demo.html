Logo Component — Full Display

This is the Bake + Brew logo component as it would appear on the website. The design replicates the real signage style with a dark grey background, bold white typography, and yellow accent color.

BAKE + BREW
PASTRIES + COFFEE

Usage

To use this logo component in your HTML, simply include the following markup:

<div class="logo"> <div class="logo__main"> <span class="logo__text">BAKE</span> <span class="logo__plus">+</span> <span class="logo__text">BREW</span> </div> <div class="logo__divider"></div> <div class="logo__subtitle">PASTRIES + COFFEE</div> </div>

Design Specifications

  • Background: Dark grey (#3a3a3a)
  • Main Text: White (#ffffff), bold uppercase, wide letter spacing
  • Plus Sign: Yellow (#f2c230), slightly heavier weight
  • Divider Line: Yellow (#f2c230), 3px height
  • Subtitle: White (#ffffff), small uppercase, extra wide letter spacing
  • Responsive: Uses clamp() for fluid typography scaling

Our Story

Bake + Brew is your local bakery in Wembley, serving fresh HALAL pasties, jacket potatoes (SPUDs), sausage rolls, slices, fresh sandwiches, paninis, a variety of confectionery items, and freshly brewed coffee. We also offer hot & iced lattes and matcha.

Located just outside Wembley Stadium on Market Square, we're the perfect spot to visit before an event at the stadium or the OVO Arena. We're open on all event days and rated #1 in Wembley on TripAdvisor!
